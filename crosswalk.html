ISO/IEC AI Standards — Summary & Crosswalk

Mobile-friendly, copyable overview with inline SVG illustrations. Optimized for iPhone viewing.

ISO/IEC 5338:2023 Lifecycle

Process model for building, operating, and improving AI systems across the lifecycle.

Why it matters: Provides the engineering “how”; plug these work products and gates into 42001.

ISO/IEC 38507:2022 Governance

Guidance for boards on roles, accountability, and oversight of AI.

Why it matters: Sets board expectations that 42001 operationalizes.

ISO/IEC 22989:2022 Concepts & Terms

Canonical AI vocabulary used across SC 42 standards.

Why it matters: Keeps policies, contracts, and training consistent.

ISO/IEC TR 24028:2020 Trustworthiness

Trust topics (transparency, robustness, privacy, safety, security) with pitfalls/mitigations.

Why it matters: Seed library for risk controls and assurance criteria.

ISO/IEC 42001:2023 AIMS

AI Management System: policies, roles, risk, audits, continual improvement.

Why it matters: Certifiable governance anchor; integrates lifecycle, trust, and impact.

ISO/IEC 42005:2025 Impact Assessment

AI impact assessment (AIIA) on individuals, groups, society across the lifecycle.

Why it matters: Standardized impact documentation and mitigations at release/change gates.
