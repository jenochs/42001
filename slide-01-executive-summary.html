üéØ Why AI Agents Are High Risk

Autonomous AI agents present unique compliance challenges:

1. Autonomous Decision-Making

Agents make decisions without human oversight, triggering strict liability under EU AI Act Article 5 (prohibited practices)

2. Data Processing at Scale

Agents process vast amounts of potentially sensitive data, creating GDPR compliance nightmares

3. Unpredictable Behavior

Emergent behaviors from agent interactions can violate transparency and explainability requirements

4. Cross-Border Operations

Agents operating globally must comply with multiple jurisdictions simultaneously
