üìã
Signed October 30, 2023: Establishes AI safety and security standards for federal agencies and contractors. Focuses on dual-use foundation models and critical infrastructure.

Key Requirements

  • Red-teaming: Mandatory for dual-use models
  • Reporting: Notify government of large training runs
  • Safety testing: Before public release
  • Watermarking: AI-generated content labeling
  • Cybersecurity: Protection against AI threats

Affected Organizations

  • Federal contractors and suppliers
  • Critical infrastructure operators
  • Healthcare and financial services
  • Companies using >10^26 FLOPS models
  • AI service providers to government
