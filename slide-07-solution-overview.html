üéØ What Are Ambient Agents?

Definition

Ambient Agents are AI systems that operate in logically isolated environments, maintaining complete separation between agents while providing comprehensive monitoring, control, and compliance capabilities.

Think of them as AI agents in "clean rooms" - they can't contaminate each other, every action is monitored, and humans maintain ultimate control.

Key Differentiators

  • Isolation by Design: Not an afterthought
  • Compliance Native: Built-in, not bolted-on
  • Observable by Default: Every action traced
  • Human-Centric Control: Override always available
  • Audit-Ready: Evidence collection automated
